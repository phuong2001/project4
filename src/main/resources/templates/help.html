HOW TO ACTIVATE THE FREE 3-MONTH DISCORD NITRO SUBSCRIPTION ON THE EPIC GAMES STORE?

We will email you an activation link valid for three (3) months of access to Discord Nitro from June 10, 2021. We will send the activation link to the email address associated with your account Epic Games. Check that the email address associated with your account is correct! Please allow 24 hours to receive the email and remember to check your spam folder.

Why can't I download more free games?

If you see this error message: " It is not possible to download other free games to your account at this time" , try the following troubleshooting steps: 1. Disable your VPN, if applicable. 2. Try using a mobile device that is not connected to the Wi-Fi network. If these two options did not resolve your issue, you will need to wait at least 24 hours before trying to get a free game from the Epic Games Store.

Why haven't I received my refund yet?

For self-refunded games or refunds requested from support, it may take up to 14 working days for the refund to be returned to your card. If you have not received a refund after this time, please contact your payment method provider who will be able to assist you. Important note: if your refund was sent to a payment method that is no longer active, please contact your bank or the supplier of your payment method.
